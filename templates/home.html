About Us

Welcome to our About Us page! We are Bata's Group, a Tech company based in Kaduna state, Nigeria. Our mission is to to create innovative, user-friendly technology that empowers people to connect and communicate in new ways. and we strive to achieve this by working on a high value on innovation, continuously seeking out new and better ways to solve problems and meet customer needs.
